# Executive AI Strategy Program Blueprint

## Program Positioning
A business leader's field guide to AI adoption.

Audience:
- Business owners
- Executives
- Operators
- Managers
- Consultants
- Non-technical decision-makers

Core transformation:
- From: Learn what AI is.
- To: Learn how to make intelligent AI business decisions.

Program outcomes:
- Navigate the AI landscape confidently
- Evaluate AI vendors and agencies with rigor
- Prioritize high-ROI opportunities
- Avoid hype, scams, and costly missteps
- Build a realistic AI adoption roadmap

   - A practical filter for distinguishing signal from noise.

   - Decision mistakes that lead to bad pilots and wasted spend.
4. Risk of Inaction vs. Risk of Bad Adoption
   - Side-by-side risk profile framework for executive planning.

Worksheets and checklists:

Case studies:

   - How to decode custom claims in proposals.

   - When wrappers add value versus margin.
7. Platform Lock-In Risks
   - How lock-in appears in contracts and integrations.
8. Product Maturity Signals
   - Reliability, support, roadmap quality, and deployment evidence.
9. Integration Readiness Snapshot
   - Practical integration fit checklist.
10. Security and Data Exposure Baseline
   - Quick risk review before pilots.
11. Landscape Scoring Worksheet
   - Compare options with weighted criteria.
12. Leadership Checkpoint
   - Defend a shortlist recommendation.

Worksheets and checklists:

Case studies:
